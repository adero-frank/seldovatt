Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Seldovatt Realtors

You can schedule a viewing by clicking the "Schedule Viewing" button on any property page, calling us at +254 723 623381, or messaging us on WhatsApp. We'll arrange a convenient time for you to view the property with one of our experienced consultants.
Our commission rates are competitive and vary depending on the property type and transaction. Typically, we charge 2-5% of the final sale price for property sales. For rentals, the commission is usually equivalent to one month's rent. Contact us for a detailed quote specific to your property.
The timeline varies depending on market conditions, property type, location, and pricing. On average, properties sell within 30-90 days. Well-priced properties in prime locations can sell much faster, sometimes within weeks. We use strategic marketing and our extensive network to expedite the process.
Yes! As registered valuers, we provide professional property valuation services for various purposes including sales, mortgages, insurance, taxation, and legal matters. Our valuations are comprehensive, accurate, and accepted by all major financial institutions in Kenya.
You'll need a valid ID or passport, KRA PIN certificate, proof of income or financing, and for non-residents, additional documentation may be required. We guide you through the entire documentation process and work with trusted lawyers to ensure a smooth, legally compliant transaction.
Yes, foreigners can buy property in Kenya, but with certain restrictions. Non-citizens can own property on leasehold titles (typically 99 years) but cannot own agricultural land. We specialize in helping international clients navigate the legal requirements and find suitable properties.
